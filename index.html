Airline case study:

  • My task is to design a new website for my client.
  • I will focus on the flight booking process: how users search for and select flights online.
  • To do this, I will go through the full UX process.

My end goal is to design and build a clickable prototype. That is testable with users, along with a detailed set of wireframes.
